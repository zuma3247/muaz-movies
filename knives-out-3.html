Movie Overview

A baffling death inside a quiet church draws Benoit Blanc into a tense investigation where faith, secrets, and suspicion blur as a close community turns against itself.

This mystery thriller kept me completely hooked. I kept trying to guess the ending, always wondering what was really going on and staying on the edge of my seat as new details were revealed.
